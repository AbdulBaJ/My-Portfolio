About Me

I’m Abdulrahman Bajabir, a Computer & Production Engineer with hands-on experience building end-to-end digital manufacturing ecosystems. My work spans Industry 4.0 implementations, drone manufacturing processes, machine vision solutions, and systems integration across IT/OT environments.

I build intelligent, connected production environments—integrating AMRs, automated quality stations, robotics, 3D printing farms, and computer-vision-driven inspection systems. My technical stack includes Python, OpenCV, YOLOv8, edge AI deployment, networked sensor systems, and industrial communication protocols.

I design and deploy ERP/MES workflows, develop ISA-95 compliant data models, and build structured manufacturing frameworks that connect production orders, routing, work centers, and real-time shop-floor telemetry. My focus is bridging engineering, software, and operations to enable scalable, high-reliability manufacturing systems.
